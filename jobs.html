Backend Web Developer
Job Description:

Help MortgageHippo improve our next-generation mortgage application portal. Our product involves several important stages:

  • Assessing our customer's needs through an interactive interview process
  • Recommending loan programs based on compatibility
  • Providing a single interface to manage all aspects of the mortgage application process, with a focus on document management

The focus of this position will be to work on item number 3. This will involve:

  • Creating a document management workflow tree with different steps based on user characteristics, such as location, property type, etc
  • Creating a flexible customer facing web interface to collect inputs for documents
  • Creating an internal company interface for agents to view and modify data, and perform management tasks including creating new document templates and document workflows
  • Automatically filling in the document templates based on the data collected
  • Exporting documents to multiple formats
  • Integrating with third party systems to collect supplemental data
  • Exporting documents and data to third party systems

The developer will work directly with our CTO to plan, execute, test, and deploy these features.


Required Skills and Personality Traits:
  • Undergraduate computer science degree and 3-4 years experience working as a computer programmer / web developer
  • Experience working in the Ruby on Rails framework, and being very comfortable with the MVC (model/view/controller) design pattern
  • Experience working on the full stack of web development, with a focus on back-end development. (database structure and migrations, rails application configuration, routes, controllers, testing, validations, security)
  • Bonus: experience securing a Rails application against unauthorized access
  • Bonus: experience integrating back-office systems with a Rails application 
  • Bonus: experience scaling a Rails application to meet high traffic demands
  • Bonus (not required at all): experience with mortgage pricing and/or mortgage application systems such as Calyx

If you think you would be a good fit at MortgageHippo, drop us an email at info@mortgagehippo.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.

MortgageHippo Mortgage Guide
Job Description:

A MortgageHippo Mortgage Guide (“MG”) combines the competencies of an originator and a processor to deliver an efficient mortgage loan experience for our clients. Rather than spending their days selling interest rates and making endless calls to prospects, MGs leverage our proprietary self-service user interface to focus on being advisors to our clients and company advocates to the industry.

The MG guides the borrower through the origination process – from the standardized processing stage until the loan file has been cleared for closing by the underwriter – providing as much assistance as needed. The MG is the one and only point of contact for borrowers.

If you are or were previously a mortgage processor and thought that your job would have been SO much easier if the loan officer actually knew what documents you need to submit the loan file to underwriting, the MG position is for you. If you are passionate about helping people navigate through the twists and turns of the mortgage loan process and want to work with a bunch of like-minded people in a dynamic start-up environment, this position is for you.


Required Skills and Personality Traits:
  • Recent experience processing conventional, FHA, VA, and jumbo loans; both purchases and refinances
  • Working knowledge of regulatory lending compliance and standards including but not limited to RESPA, HMDA, and HUD
  • Recent experience in general processing duties, but not limited to the collection of documents, analyzing of credit reports, purchase contracts, verifying employment, ordering title, and other required documents and activities needed to clear file to close
  • Minimum two years of processing experience (knowledge of Calyx Point a plus)
  • Understanding of mortgage broker process flow 
  • Patience and consultative selling skills
  • Willingness to be trained and openness to change
  • Four year undergraduate degree from an accredited university
  • This position requires S.A.F.E. registration at the time of employment.
  • Currently holds, or has the ability to obtain, the IL Mortgage Originator License, through the Nationwide Mortgage Licensing System (NMLS), which is required to perform duties of the Mortgage Guide.

If you think you would be a good fit at MortgageHippo, drop us an email at info@mortgagehippo.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.
