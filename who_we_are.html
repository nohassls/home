Who We Are

It’s about time someone made a difference

MortgageHippo was born out of total disappointment with the mortgage industry. Having lived through a mortgage crisis that caused panic and financial turmoil around the world, we decided homebuyers deserved to be treated with fairness and respect. That’s why we created a home buying experience like no other.

MortgageHippo is here to bring simplicity, transparency, and efficiency to an outdated mortgage process

Buying a home is a huge deal. It's the largest transaction most people make in their lifetime, and getting a mortgage is a very important part of the process. But for millions of people every year, the experience of getting their dream home quickly turns into a nightmare.

We’re here to change that. We believe that buying a home or refinancing into a better home loan should be an exciting part of your life’s journey, not an obstacle.

Think of MortgageHippo as a homebuyer’s personal guide. We walk you through every step of the mortgage transaction and empower you with the necessary tools to make smarter decisions. We provide unbiased recommendations and personalized results combined with loan options from our network of trusted lending partners to make sure you avoid the common pitfalls of the mortgage process and find your way home along the path of least resistance.

This is your team

Every person working at MortgageHippo has been assigned one single task: making your life easier. We love our customers, and that’s reflected in everything we do. From our Mortgage Guides to our product engineers, everyone plays an important role in building a product that’s tailored to your needs.

Based in Chicago, Illinois, we’re a tight-knit group of dedicated individuals who are constantly challenging and inspiring each other to be better, to strive for excellence, and to be passionate about what we do. We hope that when you use MortgageHippo you can tell how much love went into it.
