Who We Are

It’s about time someone made a difference

Our Mission

Nohassls.com (pronounced ‘nohassles’) was established on the belief that searching and buying insurance of all kinds shouldn’t be too time-consuming and giving away too much personal data before you made a choice.

Our goal is to re-imagine the way you think about insurance by offering an online portal (www.nohassl.com) where you control your personal information, time frame you want the quote and let companies compete for your business. It’s like a first date.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be your go-to source for all your insurance quotes. We set out to inspire you who share our passion for simpler, faster, hassle-free insurance quotes – in your time frame. Your time and personal information is precious. That is why, we want you want to be in control.

Learn more about how NoHassls works

Our Promise

Our online portal is designed to be easy to use, secure, fast and provide you with transparency of quotes to enrich your experience and put you in control of your precious time and data. We strive on providing you with a hassle-free experience.

This is your team

Every person working at NoHassls has been assigned one single task: making your life easier. We love our customers, and that’s reflected in everything we do.

Based in Sydney we are a start-up that helps people getting insurance quotes faster by setting a time frame for the insurance companies to compete for their business, without giving away too much personal data.
