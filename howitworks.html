How It Works

Learn about Your Options

To start, we'll ask a set of questions to give you a better idea of what you can expect in terms of your mortgage options. This will give you a head start on your home buying process and help us build a customized mortgage experience for you.

“MortgageHippo is very different from other mortgage sites. It’s easy to navigate and very approachable. Plus, it makes you think about everything that needs to be considered before applying for a loan.” – Mickey O., Cincinnati, OH

Get Pre-qualified

Our Affordability Report will let you know what you can expect to qualify for and identify any issues to address before getting your loan. You'll be able to download a pre-qualification letter from our lending partners to increase your negotiating power as you place an offer on a home.

Coordinate Your Team

As you begin to apply for your mortgage, you can manage your progress online and start building a team of professionals who will help you complete your home purchase. If we need anything from your team, we'll be able to reach out to them directly to save you the hassle.

“MortgageHippo is extremely helpful and very simple to follow. I could forecast different outcomes where my savings/debt would be at a point in the future and determine whether I'm better off contributing the minimum student loan payment to build my down payment savings.” – Eric M., Medford, MA

Complete Our Smart Loan Application

At this point, you will have already provided nearly all of the necessary information to apply. Based on your particular circumstance, we'll ask you for a few remaining details to complete and submit your application.

“Amazing how much you guys have simplified and improved the experience of going through the mortgage process. I can't wait to use it again when we buy our next investment property.” – Matt Z., Chicago, IL

Prepare for a Smooth Closing

Once your loan is cleared for closing, you'll only be one step away from receiving your funds from one of our lenders. We'll let you know what needs to be prepared in order to have an effortless closing experience.
