How It Works

Create your unique profile

To start, we'll ask a set of questions to allow you to build up a unique profile. You know how you seem to always enter the same information into each website. We allow you to enter it only once, and then send this data to multiple insurers with just a click of a button. Dont waste time repeating yourself any longer.

NoHassls is very different from other insurance sites. It’s easy to navigate and very approachable. Plus, it makes you think about everything that needs to be considered before applying for insurance. No more just renewing as its too time consuming to arrange new insurance. With NoHassls its a breeze.” – Mickey M., Wattle Grove, NSW

Get quotes without giving your information away.

Our unique privacy system means that you remain anonymous as long as you want. You choose which of your data to share, and until you say, 'yes' to an insuranc offer, you are anonymous. We generate a fake name and email so the insurance companies can only judge your quote on the information you supply them.

Coordinate Your Quotes

You decide when you want to be contacted and by whom. No more waiting on hold or call queues, you have the control. Tell them to contact you on your lunch hour, tonight when you get home, on the weekend, you decide when and how they contact you.

NoHassls is extremely helpful and very simple to follow. I could create what-if scenarios, and see how much it would save me if I parked in secure parking, or installed stronger locks on my windows. The possibilities are endless for me to get the best deal from insurers, without giving away all my information for marketing purposes.” – John H., Casula, NSW

Complete And Authorize your chosen provider to proceed

At this point, you will have already provided nearly all of the necessary information to apply. Based on your particular circumstance, we'll ask you for a few remaining details to complete and submit your application to the insurer.

“Amazing how much you guys have simplified and improved the experience of going through the insurance process. I can't wait to use it again when we buy our next investment property.” – Darren M., Wolli Creek, NSW

Prepare for a Smooth Process

Once your application is sent, we step out of the way. We dont get any fancy commission or a slice of your premium. We charge the insurer a flat fee, and then we are done. Its between you and your insurance company then. You can rate the experience and the deal and use this information to find better providers or deals. Remember its totally free and the power is in YOUR hands.

Whether your a new home owner or just want to update your current insurance to a better deal, NoHassls can assist you every step of the way.
